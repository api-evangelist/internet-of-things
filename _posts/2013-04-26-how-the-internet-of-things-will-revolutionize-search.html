Very soon, were going to have more data than we will know what to do with, and it will come from the world around us.As mobile devices dictate the terms of search and how results are being conveyed to end users, theres another phenomenon that will greatly influence the future of search - very soon, were going to be swimming in more data than we will know what to do with.The rise of the Internet of Things means billions of physical objects will soon generate massive amounts of data 24 hours a day.Not only will this make traditional search methods nearly impossible to use, it will also create an environment where instead of looking for things in the world, those things will be seeking us out to give us all sorts of information that will help us fix, use or buy them.(See also Forget Searching For Content - Content Is About To Start Searching For You.
