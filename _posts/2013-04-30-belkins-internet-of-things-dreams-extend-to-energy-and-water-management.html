Summary: Belkin on Tuesday launched another set of products aimed at the internet of things.This time it released an electricity and water monitoring system that uses sensors and algorithms acquired from Zensi.Belkin, the maker of myriad Apple accessories, USB widgets and even the WeMo connected outlets, has a big business in industrial products as well.And today it announced a series of sensors aimed at helping commercial companies and utilities better manage electricity and water usage.It has a pilot project with the Department of Defense related to the electric sensor technology as well as an exclusive partnership on the water side with HydroPoint Data Systems, a company that helps companies analyze and monitor water usage.
