Summary: Proteus Digital Health, the maker of an ingestible sensor that can be used to track whether patients have taken their medication, has raised $62.5 million from investors, including Oracle.Forgetting whether you took your meds will be a thing of the past if Proteus Digital Health has its way. Last year, the Redwood, Calif.company received FDA clearance for a ”digital pill” that tracks whether patients are taking their medication and how their bodies are responding to it.
