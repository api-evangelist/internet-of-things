By WARREN BUCKLEITNER As more of the very young reach for their parents’ smartphones, toy companies are responding with motion-sensing, app-infused toys like the Fisher-Price Apptivity Gym ($35, www.fisher-price.com).Here’s how this particular toy works.After you download one of the free Apptivity apps and adjust the timer and music settings, you snap your phone or iPod Touch into a child-proof container attached to the plastic “gym’’ on which the child plays.
