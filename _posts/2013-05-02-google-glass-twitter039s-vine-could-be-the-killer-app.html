Yes, Google Glass needs a killer app.Beyond the breathless hype by white guys in Silicon Valley, what exactly is the mass market supposed to do with Google Glass?The most talked-about Glass uses, like augmented reality and instant data presentation, dont have obvious appeal outside of the early adopter community.  (See also 10 Compelling Ways People Plan To Use Google Glass.) Vine on Glass, however, could be something almost everyone could get into.
