SAN FRANCISCO — Google’s wearable computer, the most anticipated piece of electronic wizardry since the iPad and iPhone, will not go on sale for many months.The glasseslike device, which allows users to access the Internet, take photos and film short snippets, has been pre-emptively banned by a Seattle bar.Large parts of Las Vegas will not welcome wearers.West Virginia legislators tried to make it illegal to use the gadget, known as Google Glass, while driving.“This is just the beginning,” said Timothy Toohey, a Los Angeles lawyer specializing in privacy issues.
