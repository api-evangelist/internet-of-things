We’re pleased to announce our new partnership with Dell.The technology leader will offer MakerBot Replicator Desktop 3D Printers, the MakerBot Digitizer Desktop 3D Scanner, and MakerBot Filament as part of an impressive portfolio of end-to-end 3D design solutions geared at enabling engineers, architects, designers, and inventive start-ups with the latest prototyping tools.We’re excited that MakerBot products will help unlock the creativity of even more small and medium-sized businesses that can benefit most from more efficient and affordable rapid prototyping.So are the folks at Dell.“With the addition of MakerBot 3D Printers and Scanners to Dell’s workstation portfolio, our customers can now bring their innovative prototypes to life much more quickly and affordably,” said Andy Rhodes, executive director, Dell Precision workstations.
