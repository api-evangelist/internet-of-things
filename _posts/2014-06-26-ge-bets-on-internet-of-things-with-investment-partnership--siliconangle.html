Planning to foster more than 30 start-ups, General Electric Co.and Frost Data Capital have partnered to found the Frost I3 Industrial Internet incubator.Frost I3 is charged with no small task: to nurture massive change in global industry and to cultivate the adoption and innovation of the Internet of Things (IoT).Basing their venture on the needs of their shared customers, GE and Frost Data Capital will use Frost I3 to explore specific IoT ideas generated by the leadership in both companies.With a variety of small companies percolating their plans, GE will have the freedom to essentially grow IoT startups that match its needs.
