The mobile revolution flipped testing on its side.Suddenly teams and testers had to think about connectivity in new ways, power sources, the impact of touch technology, system interruptions, bandwidth, and dropped connections — which was barely on the radar screen before.Testing is on the cusp of a new revolution; this time, it is the internet of things, or IoT.What Is the IoT, and Why Does It Matter While phones and tablets are mobile, they are essentially still “just” personal computers in a new size.The Internet of Things covers other things connected to the internet, like your thermostat, automobile, or washing machine.
