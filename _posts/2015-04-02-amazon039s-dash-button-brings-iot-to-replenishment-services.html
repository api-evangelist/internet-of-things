Amazon introduced the Dash Button this week in a move that brings the Internet of Things to replenishment services.The physical button attaches to a household surface and allows a user to reorder consumable goods (e.g., laundry detergent, paper towels, trash bags) from Amazon with a single push of the button.While the button provides physical ease, the true magic of Amazons new offering stems from the underlying API-driven platform that enables the Dash Button: Amazons Dash Replenishment Service (DRS).
